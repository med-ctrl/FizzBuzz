A Number Game of Divisibility

Game rules

If the number is divisible by 3, click on "Fizz".

If the number is divisible by 5, click on "Buzz".

If the number is divisible by both 3 and 5, click on "FizzBuzz".

The game continues until you fail 3 times or until you decide to stop.
